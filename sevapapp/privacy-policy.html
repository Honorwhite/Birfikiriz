Privacy Policy

Your journey of self-reflection is deeply personal. At SpiritReflect, we hold your trust and privacy as our highest priority. This policy outlines how we protect your spiritual data.

01. Introduction

Welcome to SpiritReflect. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us at privacy@spiritreflect.com.

When you use our mobile application and our services, you trust us with your personal information. We take your privacy very seriously. In this privacy notice, we seek to explain to you in the clearest way possible what information we collect, how we use it and what rights you have in relation to it.

02. Data Collection

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the App, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and Services.

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Spiritual Logs: Your self-reflection entries, goals, and progress charts.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our features to improve app performance.
verified_user

Google OAuth & Data Usage

SpiritReflect uses Google OAuth services to provide a seamless authentication experience and cross-device synchronization.

What we access

We only access your Google primary email address and basic profile information (name, profile picture).

How we use it

This data is used exclusively to sync your reflections across devices and verify your account identity.

Note: SpiritReflect does not sell your Google data to third parties. We do not use your personal information for advertising purposes.

03. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, despite our safeguards and efforts to secure your information, no electronic transmission over the Internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

lock_reset

End-to-End Encryption

Your reflection entries are encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard AES-256 encryption protocols.

04. User Rights

In some regions (like the European Economic Area), you have certain rights under applicable data protection laws. These may include the right (i) to request access and obtain a copy of your personal information, (ii) to request rectification or erasure; (iii) to restrict the processing of your personal information.

You can manage your data directly through the "Account Settings" section of the app, where you can export your data in JSON format or permanently delete your account.
